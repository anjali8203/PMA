How to Use Our App

Getting Started with Your Travel Project
  1. Create a Project: Click on "Create a project" from your dashboard. Upload your travel itinerary or start from scratch.
  2. Join a Project: If you're traveling with friends, ask them for the project code and use it to join their travel plan.
  3. Edit Your Itinerary: Add destinations, activities, and important details to your travel plan.
  4. Collaborate: Invite others to view or edit your project for group planning.
  5. Access Travel Tips: Use our built-in travel guide for destination information and travel advice.
  6. Stay Updated: Posts are continuously changing so please keep watch!
